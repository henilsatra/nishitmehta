"Design today is beyond the agency of the individual, it lies in collaboration between designers, companies and agents of design."

Nishit Mehta

Architect and Interior Designer

Nishit Mehta has been working in the architecture and interior design industry for more than 30 years. He completed his architectural education from L.S. Raheja and been based out of Mumbai ever since. The initial apprenticeship with Nikhil Adiacha and Associates, A.V Vora and associates, Ajit Bhuta and Associates, Doshi Bhatt and associates guided his further trajectory. After an early period of work on township projects like Reliance Petroleum at Jamnagar, Airoli Railway station, Call centres for WNS, he moved into the interior corporate industry. Having enjoyed working with multiple practises on different projects, today Ar. Nishit Mehta runs an independent practise while providing architectural consultancy and project coordination services to Adrianse Group(DEL), PS2 (DEL), Mindstudio(BLR), Irani Architects(BOM), SLM Architects(BOM) on various projects.
